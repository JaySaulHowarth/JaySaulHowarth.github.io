AUTONOMOUS NAVIGATION

This was my final year project when studying at The University of Newcastle, it was a long and stressful 6 months of work that included major setbacks but overall was one of the most valuable learning experiences of my life. The final product was a small raspberry pi based vehicle that could use QR codes to navigate, akin to many warehouse robots seen today.
